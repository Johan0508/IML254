Family Background

Our family consists of five members: my late father, mother, two sisters, and me. We are a close-knit family that enjoys spending time together, whether it's having meals, going on vacations, or simply chatting at home.

Family Activities

We love engaging in various activities. On weekends, we often go hiking or visit new places. During the holidays, we prefer to travel and explore new cultures and cuisines. Family game nights are also a tradition, where we play board games or watch movies together.

Values and Traditions
